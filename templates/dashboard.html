Understanding the Confusion Matrix

In Machine Learning, we don't just look at "right" or "wrong." We look at True Positives (correctly catching a bully) and False Positives (accidentally flagging a safe message). Our SVM model is tuned to minimize False Positives to ensure freedom of speech while maintaining safety.
