Help & Support Center

You are not alone. If you are a victim of cyberbullying, follow these steps immediately.

What should you do?

1
Do Not Retaliate

Responding with more anger often escalates the situation. Stay calm and do not engage with the bully.

2
Collect Evidence

Take screenshots of the messages, comments, or posts. Note the date, time, and social media handles of the harasser.

3
Report and Block

Use the platform's internal reporting tools (Instagram, Facebook, X). Block the user immediately to prevent further contact.

4
Talk to Someone

Reach out to a trusted friend, family member, or a professional counselor. Mental health is the priority.
