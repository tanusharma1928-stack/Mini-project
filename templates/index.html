Understanding the "Confidence Score"

The percentage score represents how closely the input text aligns with the patterns found in our training dataset.

  • 80-100%: High certainty. The model is very confident in its classification.
  • 50-79%: Moderate certainty. The text might contain ambiguous slang.
  • Below 50%: Low certainty. Suggests further human review.
